About the Department

The Software Engineering Department prepares students for careers in software development, systems architecture, quality assurance, and research. Our curriculum balances theory with hands-on labs and team-based projects.

Programs Offered

BSc Software Engineering — 4 years MSc Software Engineering — 2 years Diploma — 1 year

Core areas: Software Design, Testing & QA, Databases, Cloud & Distributed Systems, Mobile and Web Development.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Project-based capstone with industry mentors
  • Courses in Algorithms, Software Architecture, and DevOps
  • Practical labs: testing automation, CI/CD pipelines, cloud deployment
  • Electives in AI, Cybersecurity, Mobile & Web engineering

Labs & Research
